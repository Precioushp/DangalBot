About Dangal Bot

Dangal Bot is a leading platform...

Dangal Bot is a chatbot developed to facilitate students with their concerns regarding academic announcements. The chatbot is intended to deliver information about crucial dates, deadlines, and changes to academic agendas. It strives to improve communication between students and the universities administration, keeping students informed and up to date on vital academic information.
